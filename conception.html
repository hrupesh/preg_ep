IVF Transfer

IVF is an infertility treatment in which the eggs of a woman and sperms of a man are fertilized in a Petra dish instead of a women’s body. By adding 266 days (38weeks)to the egg retrieval data you can easily calculate the due date.

  • 3 Day Fet-Add 266 days(38weeks) and subtract 3 days
  • 5 Day Fet-Add 266 days(38weeks) and subtract 5 days.

    Ultrasound or sonogram scans are totally safe and painless procedures. As they use a high frequency sound waves to get an image. It easily evaluates fatal development. The first ultrasound (Between 6-10week) in your pregnancy is considered most accurate method to predict your due date. baby’s heartbeat cannot be heard on USG until 10-12 weeks. Your due date shouldn’t be changed if it is already set in the first trimester because ultrasounds that are done later are not that accurate
